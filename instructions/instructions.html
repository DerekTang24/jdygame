Projectile purgatory is a game where you have to dodge constant barrages of projectiles. The goal of the game is simple, but the game is not. Easy to learn, hard to master style.


You play as a character stuck in a building as creatures real and fantasy come through the sewers to fight you.

  • Use W to move up
  • Use S to move down
  • Use A to move left
  • Use D to move right
  • Use SHIFT to shoot
  • Win by defeating all enemies
